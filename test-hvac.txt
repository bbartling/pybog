HVAC Control Sequence for Single Zone AHU

Equipment: Single Zone Air Handling Unit with Economizer

Control Points:
- Supply Air Temperature Sensor (SAT)
- Outside Air Temperature Sensor (OAT) 
- Return Air Temperature Sensor (RAT)
- Supply Air Temperature Setpoint (SAT_SP)
- Outside Air Damper Command (OAD_CMD)
- Heating Valve Command (HV_CMD)
- Cooling Valve Command (CV_CMD)

Control Logic:
1. During occupied hours, maintain SAT_SP at 55°F
2. During unoccupied hours, maintain SAT_SP at 60°F
3. Enable economizer when OAT < RAT and OAT > 45°F
4. Heating sequence: If SAT < SAT_SP, open heating valve
5. Cooling sequence: If SAT > SAT_SP, open cooling valve
6. High temperature alarm if SAT > 65°F
7. Low temperature alarm if SAT < 50°F

Schedules:
- Occupied: Monday-Friday 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Unoccupied: All other times
