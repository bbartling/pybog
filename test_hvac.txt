Single Zone Unit (typical of 1)
Run Conditions - Scheduled:
The unit shall run according to a user definable time schedule.
Occupied mode: Maintain 75F cooling setpoint, 70F heating setpoint.
Unoccupied mode: Maintain 85F cooling setpoint, 55F heating setpoint.
Fan runs when system enabled. Cooling valve modulates to maintain setpoint.
Damper maintains minimum position during occupied hours.
